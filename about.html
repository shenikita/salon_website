About Us

More Than Just A Haircut. Learn More About Us!

Our vision is to be a leading customer centric salon chain, warmly welcoming all customers for an experience which takes them on a high in terms of quality Hair, beauty & Skin services in a hygienic, relaxed, comfortable environment to make their day fabulous.

The Beauty Bar is one of the most powerful and fastest growing salon chain brand PanIndia, that has given the hairstyling industry a new horizon. Emerging as the largest single salon chain in the country.

Since 1990

The Beauty Bar was established in January,1989 and has consistently shown year on year growth and is now a chain of 162 branches PanIndia. The Beauty Bar is on a growth path of 200 salons; currently employing over 6000 employees across India. The Beauty Bar has remained a pioneer in hair, beauty & nail services and is now setting new bench marks in make up exceeding all industry standards to give professionalism a new art form.

1000+ clients

Our commitment is to always try to master the art of exemplary services & experience; it is our endeavor to serve with excellence. We feel under oath to provide an unwinding environment with precision, detail and purpose where you can immerse yourself in peace and luxury. We promise to deliver with consummate skill and impeccable ambience on every visit.
